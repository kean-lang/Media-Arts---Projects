⚙️ Setting (When, Where, Who, What)

Time : Late Afternoon to Early Morning | 6:00 PM - 3:00 AM (Filipino Time / UTC+8)
Location : Cebu City, Cebu, Philippines

👥 Characters :

  • Main :
    1. Robert - The oldest (by month) in the group at siya yung nag-gusto pumunta sa Mansion. He is a dare-devil, brave and bold, but lacks enough intelligence to make the right decisions. He is quite pushy and doesn’t care about safety. Detail: He is a Cebuano that isn’t fluent throught tagalog but tries to. He has been in allot of relationships before. He sits at the middle-class of society. He also has an admiration to Cara, for being cute.
    2. David - The middle-child (by month) of the group, sarcastic and gives jokes allot, pero seryoso kapag malapit si crush, Hannah. He acts na parang wala siyang pake, but he does. Detail: He is poor but lies to the group that he has money to fit in, he hasn’t been in a relationship before and he is soft, but never reveals any secrets. He suffers from ADHD.
    3. Cara - The most soft at yung bunso (by month) in the group with her having a secret crush on David but will soon regret it. Detail: She was traumatized by the death of her mother and now lives with her abusive father, therefore, she has this soft and desciplined behavoir. She suffers depression and is acoustic or cringe. She sits at the lower middle class of society.
    4. Maria - The smartest on the group and the 2nd bunso, having knowledge through horror films at galing sa imaginative mind niya, malakas siyang babae, and she is not soft when it comes to feelings. Detail: She is a Cebuano that was taken care of a rich family there, she was happy and spoiled but she had a brother and they always fight, with her parents taking her brother’s side. Siya ay upper middle-class at mahilig siya sa Sci-fi at horror.
    5. Hannah - She is the most beutiful in the classroom but with the lowest intelligence in the group of girls. She is chased by many boys at school at flexes this to the others. She knows manipulation and is more fluent in english than tagalog. Detail: She used to live on Manila but her parents always go to Cebu, so doon muna sila hanggang tumapos si Hannah sa pag-aaral. seryoso siya lagi, pero naga joke din minsan. She is at the upper middle class of society.
  • Static & background : Robert's Mom, Other Classmates, Classmate-1, Classmate-2

Inspiration : The Villalon Mansion on Cebu - A hill side mansion that has been rumored to be haunted.

🎭 Theme: Horror, Drama, Fantasy

Concept : They will all be entering the mansion, puno ng greed at pagkagustong kuhain lahat ng kayamanan sa gusaling iyon, bigla silang nagsi-harapan sa mga Monsters & Spirits.

🧠 Plot Summary

  • Limang graduates ay umusap na pumunta sa isang haunted mansion in the night, the first day they were carefull and tried to be non-destructive to the building and its perimeter. Nung second na araw sila nagka-mali dahil hindi na nila na-ingatan ito sa kanilang greed at pagka-gusto sa yayamanin.
  • Merong Field na tinatawag na “Sphera Daemonium Clausa” or Sphere of Sealed Demons", ito’y great and old to thier current time and is still powerfull enough to keep the spirits and monsters sealed, but good turns to bad nung biglang bumalik ulit sila at may nagalaw silang bagay na parti nito na tinatawag na “Vinculum”. At sa puso ng Mansion is the “Sigilum”, yung pinaka gitna at main seal sa barrier na gawa ng mga taong tawag sakanila ay “Castodes”.
  • Ang Mansion ay gawa sa batong matigas at mayamanin, lahat ng bintana ay naka sara at naka-barricade para ma-iwasan ang mga Trespassers sa gusaling di dapat inuulit. Ito ay mga ilang taon na, umaabot ng 75 years old nato simula nung 1950s.
  • Wala lamang sa kanilang ma titira at pagka-lipas ng isang gabi natataka ang ina ni Robert dahil hindi niya nakikita yung anak niya. Pagka-alala nito na nung isang araw nagpaalam ito na gumala sa labas, at ang unang nakita ng ina ni Robert ay yung mansion, so She got to where the Mansion is and there She was sad to see that the Barrier She was part in to create was destroyed.
  • The five of them were all not from cebu, maliban kay Robert at Maria. Lahat sila (maliban sa dalawa) ay taga ibang lugar at doon lang sila napa aral dahil sa mga komplekasyons sa buhay.
